Registration is closed.

Registration for HC2 2013
Registration fees are 5.00 CHF per contestant.

The origanizers will try to form balanced 3-person teams (to the best of their abilities) out of all individuals and 2-person-teams.

The submission deadline is 8 hours after the corresponding registration deadline.
Contestant 1
We will use your e-mail address exclusively for communication relating to this and next year's contest. We will not transmit your e-mail address to our sponsors without your explicit consent.
Members of VIS, and contestants of SOI (Swiss Olympiad in Informatics) can get their ticket reimbursed up to the Halbtax rate (VIS: up to 71.00 CHF). Please consider buying a 1-day travelpass from your commune instead (~44 CHF).
If you graduated during the Fall semester 2012, and are not currently working in a IT-related company, you may still register as a student.
The after-contest dinner will take place in the Bella Vita Pizzeria near the Lausanne train station.
